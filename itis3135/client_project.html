Page Design

  1. Home
    1. Name: index.html
    2. Purpose: General information of the artist and site
    3. Audience: Anyone web surfing for art
    4. Content: Some sort of slideshow of photos of the artist and her work
    5. User input: No
    6. Validations: No
    7. Buttons or links: Navbar at the moment
    8. Actions: Maybe enlarge the slideshow
  2. About
    1. Name: about.html
    2. Purpose: Detailed information about Kayla and why she got into art
    3. Audience: Anyone that wants more information on the artist looking for why she does it
    4. Content: Mostly text and images, maybe a slideshow of Kayla
    5. User input: No
    6. Validations: No
    7. Buttons or links: Navbar
    8. Actions: No
  3. Gallery
    1. Name: gallery.html
    2. Purpose: This page will display the art that Kayla created
    3. Audience: Anyone that wants to see what she's drawn or made in the past
    4. Content: A slideshow of photos of art created and description of them
    5. User input: No
    6. Validations: No
    7. Buttons or links: Arrows that scroll thru slideshow and Navbar
    8. Actions: Maybe enlarge the slideshow
  4. Commissions
    1. Name: commissions.html
    2. Purpose: This page will give customers the option to purchase personal requested art drawings
    3. Audience: Anyone who wants to request a specific drawing from Kayla
    4. Content: Shows pricing and categories of art that she will do
    5. User input: Yes
    6. Validations: Yes
    7. Buttons or links: Yes
    8. Actions: Form submission, droplinks, input fields
  5. Contact
    1. Name: contact.html
    2. Purpose: To contact Kayla about any questions regarding her art
    3. Audience: Anyone who wants to contact Kayla
    4. Content: Shows a form that can be used to contact Kayla (input email, name, and description)
    5. User input: Yes
    6. Validations: Yes
    7. Buttons or links: Yes
    8. Actions: Form submission (input text, email, and textarea) and data sent to Kayla
