Kayla's Art Emporium

Welcome!

Welcome to my Art Emporium! I sell and take commissions to anyone that wants to acquire an art piece from me. Drawing art is a huge passion of mine and I want to extend my passion for others to see for themselves. I typically draw art during my free time and/or whenever I have to relieve any sort of stress that I am feeling.

About Me


Hello! My name is Kayla DiBruno and I am currently a freshman at Western Carolina University. I am studying to become a social worker, as I have been in and out of foster care growing up. I am 19 years old and I have found that drawing has made a lifetime impact on my mental health. It has greatly increased my creativity, as well as help pass the time or calm me down whenever I am stressed.

I chose to be in social work because I want to make a positive impact onto the children within my care. They have experienced traumatic events with their lives at home like I have, so I want to help them see how they can cope with those experiences. Many children that end up in foster care typically end up making terrible life choices; I want to prevent that as much as possible.
